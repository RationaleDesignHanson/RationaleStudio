## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Why should I invest in Zero vs. the studio (Rationale)?**
A: Zero offers concentrated exposure to a live, revenue-generating product with clear near-term liquidity potential (24-36 months). Studio investment (Rationale equity) offers diversified exposure across the entire portfolio: Zero, Atlas, Amplify, and future ventures‚Äîplus you help decide which bets we make together.

**Q: What is Rationale and how does it relate to the ventures?**
A: Rationale is the holding company (the studio) that owns equity in all portfolio ventures: Zero, Atlas, Amplify, and future companies. When you invest in the studio, you're buying Rationale equity, which gives you exposure to the entire portfolio. When you invest in Zero directly, you own Zero equity specifically.

**Q: Can I invest in Atlas or Amplify directly, not through the studio?**
A: Atlas and Amplify are built and owned by Rationale (the studio). You invest in the studio to get exposure to them. If you bring exceptional strategic value (CRE relationships for Atlas, NIL expertise for Amplify), we may negotiate direct equity or preferential studio terms based on your strategic contribution beyond capital.

**Q: What makes this different from other venture studios?**
A: Three things: (1) We're self-funded through client services ($900K run rate), removing dilution pressure. (2) AI acceleration enables 2-3 ventures per year instead of 1 every 2 years. (3) Solo founder model means no partner conflicts and total execution control. (4) Studio partners actively help decide which bets to pursue‚Äînot passive LPs.

**Q: How do you build so capital-efficiently?**
A: AI tooling + 7 years at Meta + solo founder model. Zero cost $35K to build (8 microservices, 182 Swift files, live on App Store). Atlas roadmap took 3 weeks (103KB docs). Amplify blueprint took 2 weeks (129KB docs). We leverage AI for architecture, code generation, and planning, then add Meta-grade execution discipline for production quality.

**Q: What's the exit strategy?**
A: For Zero: Target $50K MRR ($600K ARR) in 18 months, then either (1) strategic acquisition by email/productivity companies or (2) continue growing to profitability. For portfolio ventures: Mix of strategic acquisitions, revenue-based exits, and long-term holds. Studio model isn't built for quick flips‚Äîwe build businesses that compound and appreciate.

**Q: Are both Zero and Studio raising simultaneously?**
A: Yes. Priority is to secure BOTH: (1) $600K for Zero to fund 18-month runway to $50K MRR, and (2) studio partners to secure operating budget, hire engineers, and fund Atlas/Amplify builds. We're flexible on studio terms based on strategic value partners bring.
