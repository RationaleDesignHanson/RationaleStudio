### 4. **COMBINE: Business Model Section (Slides 19 + 20)**

**Current:**
- Slide 19: "Business Model" - Starter kit, refills, accessories + moat
- Slide 20: "IP Strategy" - Patent layers and defensibility

**Recommended Consolidation:**
```
New Slide 19: "Defensible Business Model"
Headline: "Razor-Blade Economics with Multi-Layer IP Moat"
Subheadline: "Format + dispenser ecosystem creates switching costs"

Left Column: Revenue Streams
• Starter Kit: Dispenser + 2 refills (~break-even CAC)
• Refills: Recurring margin engine (subscription + retail)
• Accessories: Leash clip / mini pouch / travel kit

Right Column: IP Layers
```
